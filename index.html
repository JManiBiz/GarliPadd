What is GarliPadd?

GarliPadd is an alternative method of using garlic to lower blood pressure. Garlic has many medical benefits including lowering blood pressure. However, garlic has a strong and distinct scent. This is why we created GarliPadd. All the benefits of garlic without the scent.

How is GarliPadd made?

GarliPadd is made from two simple ingredients: garlic and oilve oil. The garlic is pressed with our specialty garlic press to extract the garlic oil. This extract is higly concentrated and is mixed with olive oil to dilute it. The solution is left undisturbed overnight and then strained. 5g of the garlic oil extract is inserted into the adhesive polymer layer which is nested between the impermeable backing and the release liner.
